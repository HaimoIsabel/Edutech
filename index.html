Edtech Investment Trend in China
Investment Growth by Sub-market

Please click on the corresponding series of bars or legend to detect volume or share of each sub-market.

Edtech Investments in China has been going through a constant growth since 2015 Q1 in terms of both funding amount and numbers of deals, although there were a few fluctuations at the beginning of 2016. As the whole market scaling up, proportion of funding raised by a few sub-markets (K12, Language Learning) have shown a slightly decrease while proportion for others (Stem, Competence Edu.) have shown a steady growth. Investment Maturity by Sub-market

Clicking on the tab menus to detect situation for different years.

As the previous page breaking investments by sub-market, we develop further by introducing one more dimension—funding stage.

Specifically, this visualization work presents the distribution of Edtech investment by breaking down the number of deals by two dimensions (sub-market and funding stage). Each grid in the chart represents the number of companies funded in a certain funding stage for a specific sub-market.

The sub-markets on the y-axis are ranked by the number of deals in that year. The darker grid represents more funded deals. Accordingly, we can conclude from the total data that companies in category of K12, Language Learning, Vocational education have received more investors' attention.

If we break down the data in years, K12, and language learning attacts most investment deals mainly in angel and seed stage in 2014. Over the years, early-stage startups developed into more mature companies that attracted Series C and D rounds. Additionally, new sub-market like STEM and Saas&Informatization, which is almost blank in 2014, attracted investors'attention in 2017 and 2018.

Funding Stage by Sub-market

Please hovering on bars on the right/left sides to explore more.

If you are interested in question like how many (or how few) Edtech companies could make their way to the later round like D or E, and question like is it possible that companies in specific sub-market would be expected to have a higher rate to fight their way until to round D or E, this visualization work could help to answer these questions.

The bipartite chart is based on the date of 781 Edtech companies which got funded over the last five years. Each bar on the left side represents the proportion of companies in the particular sub-market and each bar on the right side repsents companies in their latest round. It shows that around 3.5% of them arrived at round C, around 0.9% of them make it to round D，and 38.3% and 15.1% of them are still at round seed/Angel and round Pre-A round.

By hovering on bars on the left (which represent sub-markets), we could compare situations between different sub-markets. For example, for companies in sector K12, 4.8% and 3.2% of them made their way to round C and D respectively, whereas only around 3.9% and 0% of companies in sector Vocational Edu. arrived at round C and D. Further, we could also know the composition of companies at different stage of round. This could be achieved by hovering on bars on the right side (which represent latest round).

Explanation for Each Sub-market

K12

K12 mainly refers to test prep for school subjects and entrance exams.

Higher Edu.

Higher Education mainly refers to training courses for post-graduate entrance exams or other test-prep during college years.

Vocational Education

Vocational education prepares people to work in various jobs and helps them to get vocational certificates.

Study Abroad Services

Study Abroad Services mainly deals with overseas college application and related services.

Language Learning

Language Learning helps students to build communication competence in a foreign language, mostly English.

Enrichment Program

Enrichment Program generally includes all that related to the development of a person’s intellectual, emotional, social, physical, artistic, creative and spiritual potentials.

Knowledge payment

Knowledge payment refers to product and services that create learning and knowledge content, in text, audio and video format, for life-long leaners.

SaaS & Informatization

Informatization, a term used most in public school system in China, aims to apply information technology in teaching, and improve the digital literacy of both teachers and students.

Ed SaaS Solutions allows virtual academic process management cloud to control different aspect of institutions activities.

Early Childhood Edu.

Early childhood education is to describe any type of educational programs designed to aid in the cognitive and social development of children before they enter elementary school.
